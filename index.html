Who I am

A bit about me

I'm Chraeten George Lategan — an 18-year-old first-year Bachelor of Computing student at Belgium Campus in South Africa. I grew up in Standerton, Mpumalanga, and I'm passionate about programming, technology, and solving real-world problems.

My coding journey began with Python and Delphi in high school, where I built an interest in automation and practical software. I'm currently expanding into web development and data science, always looking for the next thing to learn.

Outside of code, I'm constantly trying new things — whether that's training at the CrossFit box, pushing myself on a long run, or picking up a skateboard. Life's too short not to experiment.
